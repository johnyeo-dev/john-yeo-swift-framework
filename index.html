✶ Pace of Love ✶

Your 5-Step System: From Webinar to Thriving Group Classes

A heart-based approach to growing your tutoring business with peace, not pressure.

✶ SWIFT doesn't mean fast. It means moving forward — at a pace that is yours.
✶ The Foundation ✶

SWIFT is built on the 3C Framework

Before the steps, there is the strategy. The 3C Framework — Content, Community, Connection — is the engine behind everything SWIFT helps you build. Most educators work on only one. The ones who thrive run all three.

C1
C1 · Content
Attract the Right People
Content

The right content pulls the right people toward you before they ever reach out. Not posts about your credentials or results — but stories that make parents and students feel deeply understood. The tutor who writes about what it feels like to have a child who cries over homework will always out-attract the tutor who posts "A1 results guaranteed." Emotion creates connection. Credentials create comparison.

S
SWIFT Step
S — Signature Offer is where your Content identity begins. Your signature framework makes every post feel cohesive and positions you as the expert with a proprietary approach.
C2
C2 · Community
Convert Followers into Believers
Community

You don't need 10,000 followers. You need 100 people who genuinely trust you. A parent who attends your free webinar and feels understood is 10x more likely to enrol than one who has seen 50 of your posts. Live experience collapses the trust timeline. Shared experience turns curious followers into people who invest — not just in your programme, but in you.

W
SWIFT Step
W — Webinar is your Community catalyst. A 90-minute Heart-Based Webinar using the 70/30 formula is the single highest-leverage activity to build the trust that converts.
C3
C3 · Connection
Retain, Refer & Return
Connection

Most educators lose leads not because they can't close — but because they never follow up. A warm lead who doesn't hear from you after a webinar will forget you within 72 hours. Follow-up is not chasing. It is caring. When you follow up with genuine value — not a sales pitch — you're not being pushy. You're being the person who shows up even before someone becomes a client.

I
SWIFT Step
I — Invite & Follow-Up is your Connection system. Send your follow-up within 24 hours of every webinar — to every attendee, buyer or not. This single habit separates those who fill programmes from those who wonder why nobody converted.
The 5 Steps
S
Signature Offer

Get crystal clear on your niche, your ideal student, and the specific transformation you deliver. This positions you as the go-to expert before you run a single webinar.

Milestone
You can confidently say: "I help [who] achieve [what] in [timeframe]."
Action
Write your one-line signature offer statement.
W
Webinar

Run a free Heart-Based Webinar to deliver genuine value, build trust, and invite parents or students to take the next step. Focus on WHAT they'll gain, not HOW you teach it.

Milestone
Your first webinar goes live — real people show up and engage.
Action
Set your webinar date and send your first invite.
I
Invite & Follow-Up

Use heart-based follow-up messages to nurture every lead after the webinar. Your system converts 20–40% of inquiries into enrolled students — no pushy sales, just authentic connection.

Milestone
First replies and enquiries come in from interested families.
Action
Send your follow-up sequence within 24 hours of the webinar.
F
Fill Group Class

Transition from exhausting 1-on-1 lessons to a profitable online group class with 4–6+ paying students within 30–90 days — even if you're starting from zero.

Milestone
First students enrolled and first group class runs successfully.
Action
Confirm your first cohort and schedule your launch date.
T
Track & Relaunch

Collect testimonials, review what worked, and systematise your process. Use your clients' wins as social proof to fill your next round — bigger, faster, and with more confidence.

Milestone
Second round is planned and filling up.
Action
Request testimonials and set a date for Round 2.

SWIFT meets the Product Architect Blueprint

SWIFT is the client journey. The Blueprint is the full system. Here is how they align.

"The Product Architect Blueprint is the full map. SWIFT is how you walk it — one step at a time, at your own pace."

↓ Fill the Webinar Paid ads / organic / survey / referrals — pick what works Pillar 2 · ACCESS AI Authentic Content — attracts prospects already looking for people like you

Key Insight

Everything before the Webinar — paid ads, organic content, surveys, books, referrals — has ONE job: fill the webinar. Don't get overwhelmed by marketing choices. Pick what works for you and move at your own pace.
