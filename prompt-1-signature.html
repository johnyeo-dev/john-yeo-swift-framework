Prompt 1 of 6

Create Your Signature Programme

Instructions

Your signature programme is the cornerstone of your education business — a named, structured offer that sets you apart from every other tutor or educator in your space. This prompt uses your real student testimonials to build a framework that's authentically yours.

1
Collect your testimonials
Gather feedback from your best students and parents. Scroll through WhatsApp chats, emails, and old messages. Look for "thank you" notes, parent updates, and any message where someone described a result or transformation.
→ Aim for at least 10 testimonials
2
Compile them into a single document
Copy all testimonials into one Word doc or PDF — one testimonial per paragraph is fine. Keep it clean and readable. This file will be uploaded directly to Claude.
→ Save as "my-testimonials.pdf"
3
Open Claude and upload your document
Go to claude.ai → start a new chat → click the paperclip icon → upload your testimonials file. Then copy and paste the full prompt below into Claude.
→ Open Claude ↗
4
Paste the prompt and run it
Copy the entire prompt in the box below and paste it into Claude. Claude will analyse your testimonials and generate your personalised Signature Programme framework, including a memorable acronym, full programme structure, pricing guidance, and your first marketing message.
📋
Upload at least 10 testimonials before running this prompt

The more testimonials you provide, the more specific and powerful your framework will be. Look for these sources:

  • WhatsApp messages from parents or students
  • Google or Facebook reviews
  • Email replies saying "thank you" or sharing results
  • Screenshots of progress photos with captions
  • Comments from your social media posts
📋 Your Claude Prompt — Copy Everything Below
I am an educator / tutor and I want to create my Signature Programme — a named, structured offer that positions me as the go-to expert in my space. I have uploaded my student and parent testimonials above. Please complete the following steps using ONLY the testimonials I've provided as your primary source material: ────────────────────────────────────── STEP 1 — ANALYSE MY TESTIMONIALS ────────────────────────────────────── Read through all my testimonials and identify: 1. The core transformation I consistently deliver (what changes for students before → after) 2. My distinctive teaching approach (how do I teach differently from others?) 3. The emotional qualities mentioned repeatedly (e.g. patient, safe, encouraging, fun) 4. The specific results my students value most (confidence, grades, mindset, skills) 5. The most compelling promise I could make based on real evidence from these testimonials 6. 3–5 recurring words or phrases that show up across multiple testimonials ────────────────────────────────────── STEP 2 — CREATE MY FRAMEWORK ACRONYM ────────────────────────────────────── Based on the themes in my testimonials, create a memorable acronym that captures the essence of how I teach. This acronym will become the name of my Signature Programme. Rules for the acronym: - 4–5 letters is ideal (shorter = more memorable) - Each letter represents a stage, principle, or pillar of my teaching method - It must reflect motion, progress, or transformation - Avoid overused education acronyms: RISE, GIVE, CARE, GROW, LEAP, SPARK (unless it genuinely fits perfectly) - Each letter's meaning should be understandable by a parent or 8-year-old Give me 10 unique acronym options. For each one: a) State the acronym b) Explain what each letter stands for c) Write one sentence explaining the overall concept in plain language ────────────────────────────────────── STEP 3 — BUILD THE FULL PROGRAMME STRUCTURE ────────────────────────────────────── Choose the acronym you believe fits best from your 10 options (or wait for me to select one — you can proceed with your top recommendation). Using the chosen acronym, design the full Signature Programme: 1. Programme Name: "[ACRONYM] + a descriptive phrase" (give 3 name options) 2. Tagline: A 1-sentence description of the transformation (3 options) 3. Ideal Student: Who is this programme for? Describe them specifically — age, struggles, what they want 4. Programme Duration: Recommended weeks/months and sessions per week 5. Delivery Format: Group, 1-to-1, hybrid, online or in-person — and why this format suits my audience 6. Module Breakdown: For each letter of the acronym, describe: - What students do in this phase - What they feel and gain - Approximate timeframe 7. Signature Touch: 1 unique element of this programme that no other tutor offers (derived from my testimonials) 8. Programme Outcome Statement: Complete this sentence — "By the end of [Programme Name], your child will…" ────────────────────────────────────── STEP 4 — PRICING GUIDANCE ────────────────────────────────────── Based on the transformation described in my testimonials and the programme structure above: 1. Suggest a pricing range for this programme (per month / per term / full programme) 2. Explain the pricing rationale — why this price reflects the value, not just the hours 3. Describe 2 pricing tiers: a Standard and a Premium version of this programme ────────────────────────────────────── STEP 5 — FIRST MARKETING MESSAGE ────────────────────────────────────── Write the first marketing message I can use to introduce this programme. This should be: 1. A short Instagram / Facebook caption (150 words max) announcing my Signature Programme 2. A WhatsApp message I can send to past students/parents to invite them into this programme 3. A 1-paragraph "about the programme" bio I can use on my website or profile Use "you / your child" language, not "I / we". Write with warmth and specificity — pull phrases from the actual testimonials where possible. ────────────────────────────────────── FORMAT NOTES ────────────────────────────────────── - Present each step as a clearly labelled section - Use bullet points and bold headers for easy reading - Do not use generic language — everything should be grounded in my specific testimonials - If you notice anything surprising or powerful in my testimonials, call it out explicitly
