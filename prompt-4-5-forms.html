Leads Collection Enquiry Form

What This Creates

A Google Form built automatically via Apps Script that captures warm leads from parents interested in your programme. Copy the prompt, paste into Claude, and you'll get a ready-to-run script — no coding needed.

Form Fields
1
Parent's name
2
Child's education level
3
Contact number
4
Email address
5
Biggest challenge your child is facing right now
⚙️
How to use the Apps Script Claude generates

Go to script.google.com → New Project → paste the script → click Run. The form is created instantly in your Google Drive. Watch the video above for a step-by-step walkthrough.

📋 Prompt 5 — Copy & Paste into Claude
Please write a complete Google Apps Script that automatically creates a Google Form for collecting leads from parents interested in my tutoring or coaching programme. The form should collect the following fields: 1. Parent's name (short answer, required) 2. Child's education level (multiple choice — required) Options: Primary 1, Primary 2, Primary 3, Primary 4, Primary 5, Primary 6, Secondary 1, Secondary 2, Secondary 3, Secondary 4, Secondary 5, JC 1, JC 2, Other 3. Contact number (short answer, required) 4. Email address (short answer, required) 5. What is the biggest challenge your child is facing right now? (paragraph, required) Script requirements: - Create the entire form programmatically using Google Apps Script - Set the form title to: "Tell Me About Your Child" - Set the form description to: "I'd love to learn more about your child and how I can help. This takes about 2 minutes to fill in." - Make all questions required - Set the confirmation message (shown after submission) to: "Thank you! I'll be in touch within 24 hours." - Link the form to a new Google Sheet to collect responses automatically - Name the Google Sheet: "Leads" After the script, please also give me: 1. Step-by-step instructions: how to open Google Apps Script, paste the code, and run it 2. How to find the form link once the script has run 3. How to view responses in the connected Google Sheet 4. A short message I can send to parents (WhatsApp or Instagram DM) asking them to fill in the form

Prompt 5 of 6

Student & Parent Feedback Form

What This Creates

A feedback form that helps you understand how your students and parents feel about your programme — and surfaces testimonials, referrals, and improvement areas automatically.

Form Fields
1
How likely are you to recommend us to a friend? (1–10 scale)
2
One area where we could improve
3
What have we done well?
4
What other support would be helpful for your child?
💡
Send this at the end of every term or programme

Responses with a score of 9–10 are your best candidates to ask for a public testimonial. The improvement field often surfaces the next programme idea.

📋 Prompt 5 — Copy & Paste into Claude
Please write a complete Google Apps Script that automatically creates a feedback form for parents and students currently in my tutoring or coaching programme. The form should collect the following: 1. How likely are you to recommend us to a friend or family member? (linear scale 1–10, required) Label 1 as "Not likely at all" and 10 as "Extremely likely" 2. One area where we could improve (paragraph, required) 3. What have we done well? (paragraph, required) 4. What other support would be helpful for your child? (paragraph, optional) 5. Your name (short answer, optional — label it "Your name (optional)") Script requirements: - Create the entire form programmatically using Google Apps Script - Set the form title to: "Share Your Feedback" - Set the form description to: "Your feedback means a lot to us and helps us improve. This takes less than 2 minutes." - Set the confirmation message to: "Thank you so much for taking the time — we really appreciate it!" - Link the form to a new Google Sheet to collect responses automatically - Name the Google Sheet: "Feedback" After the script, please also give me: 1. Step-by-step instructions: how to open Google Apps Script, paste the code, and run it 2. How to view the responses in the connected Google Sheet 3. A warm WhatsApp message I can send to parents and students asking them to fill in the feedback form (keep it short, friendly, not corporate)
