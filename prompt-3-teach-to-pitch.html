How to use this prompt
1
Prepare your teaching slides
Export your webinar slides as a PDF, or take screenshots of each slide. Any format Claude can read works.
2
Upload the slides to Claude
Open Claude, click the paperclip icon, and attach your slides file before pasting the prompt.
3
Copy and paste the prompt below
Paste it into the same message as your slides. Send it and wait for Claude to ask you questions.
4
Fill in the missing details when prompted
Claude will ask for your offer details — workshop name, price, bonuses, date, and anything not on the slides. Answer each question and it will write your complete script.
🔍
Claude will probe you for missing details. It reads your slides first, then asks specifically for what it needs to complete the script — so nothing gets left as a blank placeholder.

Generate Your Webinar Pitch Script

📋 Prompt 3 — Copy & Paste into Claude
I'm a tutor preparing for a free webinar. I've uploaded my teaching slides above. I want you to help me generate a complete, ready-to-read webinar script using the exact framework below. Before writing the script, please read my slides carefully and then ask me for any details you still need. Specifically, check whether I've already provided: 1. My workshop or programme name 2. The main transformation I promise students 3. Workshop date, time and location (online via Zoom or in-person) 4. Workshop duration 5. The 3 main outcomes or things covered in the workshop 6. What participants walk away with (workbook, notes, template, etc.) 7. Regular price and early bird price 8. Maximum number of students 9. Bonuses — name each one and its individual value 10. How to register (link, QR code or DM) Ask me only for what's missing. Once you have everything, fill in all the [bracketed placeholders] in the script below and write it out in full — ready for me to read on the day. --- WEBINAR SCRIPT - READ THIS EXACTLY PART 1: TEACH (30-40 minutes) [Share your 3 teaching points with examples. Keep it conversational and valuable.] PART 2: TRANSITION After Your 3rd Teaching Point: SAY: "This third technique is just ONE of the key parts I share with my own students to help them jump from [C grade] to [A grade] within [1-2 months]." [PAUSE 3 seconds] SAY: "Give me a YES in the chat if you got value from today's sharing." [WAIT 10 seconds] SAY: "I can see many yeses coming in. That's wonderful!" [Even if no one responds, say this anyway] SAY: "Parents, is it okay if I spend the next 10 minutes sharing how you can access the REST of my complete teaching system?" [PAUSE 5 seconds] SAY: "Great! Let me share this with you..." PART 3: PITCH Step 1: Describe Your Offer SAY: "So here's what I've put together... I'm running a [Workshop Name] on [Topic], and it's happening on: Date: [November 15th, Saturday] Time: [2pm to 5pm] Where: Online via Zoom / At [location] Duration: [3 hours] In this workshop, we'll cover: [Outcome 1] [Outcome 2] [Outcome 3] You'll walk away with [workbook/notes/template]." Step 2: Qualify Your Audience SAY: "Now, I want to be very clear - this workshop is ESPECIALLY designed for parents who are: Hands-on - Involved in your child's education and willing to support at home Action-oriented - You want to implement and see real results Committed - Serious about improvement and ready to put in the work If that sounds like you, this will be a great fit." [PAUSE 2 seconds] SAY: "If you're looking for a quick fix or want the tutor to do everything... this probably isn't for you, and that's okay." Step 3: Value Stack SAY: "Here's everything you're getting: Main Workshop: [Name] - worth $250 BONUS #1: [Name] - worth $80 BONUS #2: [Name] - worth $100 BONUS #3: [Name] - worth $70 Total value: $500 Your investment today: Just $200" Step 4: Create Urgency SAY: "Now, I can only take [10] students because I want to give personalized attention. EARLY BIRD SPECIAL: For those who sign up BEFORE this call ends today, you get $50 off. Your price today: $150 But this early bird rate expires when we finish today." Step 5: Call to Action SAY: "Here's how to register RIGHT NOW: Option 1: Click the registration link I'm dropping in the chat: [DROP LINK] Option 2: Scan this QR code [SHOW ON SCREEN] Option 3: Send me a DM saying 'INTERESTED' Once you've registered and paid, screenshot and send it to me for immediate confirmation. I'm going to put a 5-minute countdown timer on the screen right now. Use this time to register, and I'll be here to answer any questions you have." [START 5-MINUTE COUNTDOWN TIMER ON SCREEN] [Stay on camera, smile, answer questions in chat, encourage registrations] WHAT TO SAY DURING THE 5 MINUTES: [After 2 minutes]: "I'm seeing some registrations coming in - that's great! We have 3 minutes left for the early bird rate." [After 4 minutes]: "One minute left for the early bird discount! If you're on the fence, now's the time. Registration link is in the chat." [When timer ends]: "Alright, the early bird period has ended. For those who registered, congratulations - you've secured your spot! For those who didn't make it in time, you can still register at the regular rate. Just DM me." WHAT IF NO ONE RESPONDS? If silence after "Give me a YES": "I can see some yeses, and I know some of you are processing. That's normal." If silence after "Is it okay if I share more?": "I'm seeing some nods and messages, so let me go ahead..." If no questions: "No questions? Great! That means I explained it clearly. Payment links are in the chat. I'm seeing some of you taking action already!" KEY REMINDERS: ✓ Read naturally - not like a robot ✓ Maintain eye contact with camera ✓ Smile when appropriate ✓ Don't apologize for pitching ✓ Be confident - you KNOW you can help ✓ If nervous, it's okay - keep going ✓ Goal: Close just 1 person = SUCCESS YOU'VE GOT THIS! 🎯
